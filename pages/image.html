Xbox - Xbox is a video gaming brand created and owned by Microsoft. The brand consists of five video game consoles, as well as applications (games), streaming services, an online service by the name of Xbox Live, and the development arm by the name of Xbox Game Studios. The brand was first introduced in the United States in November 2001, with the launch of the original Xbox console.

PlayStation - PlayStation is a Japanese video brand, a division of Sony.It consistes of home console-PlayStation series, handed console-PlayStation Portable, PlayStation games and PlayStation network. The PlayStation Network is an online service with about 110 million registered users and over 103 million active users monthly.The PlayStation Store, which allows the purchase and download of games and various forms of multimedia.The lastest PlayStation series is PlayStation5, yet not as popular as PlayStation4.

Nintendo Switch - Switch is a successful video game console developed by Nintendo and released worldwide in 2017. The console itself is a tablet that can either be docked for use as a home console or used as a portable device, making it a hybrid console.Nintendo Switch runs on a custom-made operating system. It includes quick access buttons for a News feed from Nintendo, eShop access, and a photo album for screenshots captured during gameplay.
